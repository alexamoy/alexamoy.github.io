about me

Hello World!

Alexa here, entering the big and scary world of web development :O

My road to code had many detours. I had a few encounters with coding starting at the age of 13, when I took my first programming class as an elective. After earning my bachelor's degree in Economics at the University of Maryland, College Park, I decided to do a full 180 and pursue software engineering as a career and haven't looked back.

In May 2018, I completed the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy, a 13 week immersive software engineering course where I learned and implemented JavaScript libraries and frameworks such as React, Redux, Express, and Sequelize. Now I am currently working as a Teaching Fellow for Fullstack Academy where I am further developing my programming skills while also mentoring a class of 52 motivated students.

When I'm not coding, you can find me out in the mountains on a hike, exploring New York City, and hanging out with my friends :)
